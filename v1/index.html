About

I am a senior news-editorial major at the University of Colorado with a minor in technology and additional entrepreneurial studies. However, I spend more of my time as the editor-in-chief of the CU Independent.

In the past I've been lucky enough to intern with Rich Clarkson & Associates, a multimedia firm in Denver, the Greeley Tribune as a staff photographer and in the nation's capital with the Scripps Howard Foundation Wire.

When I'm not taking on other responsibilities, I sincerely enjoy a good breakfast, exploring code, riding my bike or watching a Batman movie.

Many people ask what I want to do after I graduate and the honest answer is that I want to get a decent-paying job. Whether that means I start my own business, become a photojournalist or code for the rest of my life is TBD.
